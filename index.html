The Problem

Digital inequality is locking out millions of rural learners in South Africa from the future.

  • Only 22% of rural schools have reliable access to the internet.
  • Loadshedding disrupts learning almost daily.
  • Most coding programs assume every child has a laptop. We don’t.

Our Solution: The Unplugged Code Framework™

We teach real coding logic, no internet required. Hands-on, device-optional activities teach core programming concepts.

The Pilot: 2025–2026

Site: Zingisa Primary School, Eastern Cape, South Africa
Duration: 12 months
Reach: 60 learners, 4 trained teachers, 20 laptops in rotation

This is a prototype for national transformation.

The Movement Map

  1. Phase 1 – Zingisa Pilot: Build, test, refine Unplugged Code Framework.
  2. Phase 2 – Train 5 More Schools (2026): Launch with 100+ learners and local clubs.
